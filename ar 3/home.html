Messi

As the Messi family had relatives in Catalonia, they sought to arrange
a trial with Barcelona in September 2000. First team director Charly Rexach
wanted to sign him, but the board of directors hesitated;at the time it was highly
unusual for European clubs to sign foreign players of such a young age.
